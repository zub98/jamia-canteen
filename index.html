Our Story

A.R. CATERERS is a vibrant student-focused canteen located in the heart of Jamia Millia Islamia university campus. With our spacious outdoor seating areas, we serve fresh, delicious and affordable meals to students and faculty alike. Our menu features a diverse selection of Indian and international cuisines, fresh juices, snacks and beverages. We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and food quality while keeping our prices student-friendly.
